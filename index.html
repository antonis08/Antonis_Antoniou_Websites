What I Can Do For You

The Online Launchpad

Perfect for: Businesses taking their first step online, or those who need a solid digital business card.

What you get:

  • 1-3 Essential Pages: I'll craft a concise yet impactful website with pages like your Homepage, About Us, and a Services or Contact page. Think of it as your primary online information hub.
  • Mobile-Ready Design: Your site will look fantastic and function flawlessly on any device – phones, tablets, and desktops. No pinching or zooming required!
  • Search Engine Ready: We'll lay the groundwork to help search engines like Google understand what your business is about, making it easier for customers to find you.
  • Direct Contact Form: A simple, built-in way for visitors to reach out to you directly from your website.
  • Social Media Connections: Seamlessly link your website to your existing social media profiles, helping you build a stronger online community.

This plan is ideal for: Consultants, freelancers, home-based businesses, or anyone needing a professional and reliable online presence without any fuss.
